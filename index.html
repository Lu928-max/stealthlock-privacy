1. INTRODUCTION

StealthLock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app locker application collects, uses, and protects your information.

StealthLock is designed to provide security and privacy for your mobile device by allowing you to lock applications and detect unauthorized access attempts.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

StealthLock collects and processes the following information locally on your device:

a) App Usage Information

  • List of installed applications on your device
  • App usage statistics to detect when locked apps are accessed
  • This information is used solely to provide app locking functionality

b) Camera/Photos

  • Photos captured when incorrect PIN attempts are made (Intruder Detection feature)
  • These photos are stored locally on your device only
  • You have full control to view and delete these photos at any time

c) Security Credentials

  • Your chosen PIN or biometric data for authentication
  • Stored securely and encrypted on your device only

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To lock and unlock applications based on your settings
  • To capture photos of unauthorized access attempts (Intruder Detection)
  • To authenticate you when accessing locked apps
  • To display statistics about app locking activity

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

IMPORTANT: All data collected by StealthLock is stored LOCALLY on your device only.

  • We DO NOT upload any data to external servers
  • We DO NOT share your data with third parties
  • We DO NOT have access to your data
  • Photos captured by intruder detection remain on your device
  • All data is deleted if you uninstall the app

Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption methods.

5. PERMISSIONS REQUIRED

StealthLock requires the following permissions to function:

a) Usage Access (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS)

  • Required to: Monitor when apps are opened to trigger lock screen
  • Why needed: Core functionality of app locking

b) Camera (CAMERA)

  • Required to: Capture photos during unauthorized access attempts
  • Why needed: Intruder detection feature
  • User control: This feature can be enabled/disabled in settings

c) Storage (READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES)

  • Required to: Save intruder photos locally
  • Why needed: Store captured photos for your review
  • User control: You can delete photos at any time

d) Display Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)

  • Required to: Show lock screen over other apps
  • Why needed: Core functionality of app locking

e) Biometric Authentication (USE_BIOMETRIC)

  • Required to: Use fingerprint/face unlock
  • Why needed: Convenient authentication method
  • User control: Optional, can use PIN instead

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

StealthLock does NOT integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. Your data stays completely private on your device.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

StealthLock is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

8. DISGUISE FEATURE

StealthLock includes a "disguise" feature that allows the app to appear as a calculator or notes app in your app drawer.

  • This feature is designed for privacy protection
  • The app still functions as StealthLock when accessed with your PIN
  • No deceptive practices are used - you, the device owner, control this feature

9. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the app
  • Delete any or all data at any time
  • Disable any features (such as camera/intruder detection)
  • Uninstall the app and remove all data

10. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

11. DATA RETENTION

All data is retained locally on your device until:

  • You manually delete it
  • You uninstall the app (all data is automatically deleted)
